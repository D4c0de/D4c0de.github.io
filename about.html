About Me

Hello! I'm Marcin, a passionate programmer with a strong curiosity for how things work and a drive to create solutions that make life easier. My journey in programming began a few years ago, and ever since, I've been exploring the intricacies of coding, primarily in C++ and C#. From building console applications to working on desktop tools, I enjoy turning ideas into reality through code.

Beyond programming, I have a solid foundation in database management and web development. I find joy in learning and tackling new challenges, especially in areas like version control (Git) and collaborative projects. My knack for communication and teamwork has been honed through various experiences, both professional and personal.
